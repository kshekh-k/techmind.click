<h2>What Is a Text Case Converter?</h2>

<p>
A <strong>Text Case Converter</strong> is a simple online tool that helps you change
the capitalization style of your text in one click. Instead of manually editing
each word or sentence, you can instantly convert text into different formats.
</p>

<p>
This is especially useful for writers, developers, students, content creators,
and SEO professionals who work with large amounts of text every day.
</p>

<h3>Common Case Conversion Options</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>UPPERCASE</strong> – Converts all letters to capital letters</li>
  <li><strong>lowercase</strong> – Converts all letters to small letters</li>
  <li><strong>Sentence case</strong> – Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence</li>
  <li><strong>Title Case</strong> – Capitalizes the first letter of each word</li>
  <li><strong>Toggle Case</strong> – Switches uppercase to lowercase and vice versa</li>
</ul>

<h2>What Is Text Formatting?</h2>

<p>
<strong>Text formatting</strong> helps you clean, organize, and structure your text
to make it more readable and professional. This is useful when preparing content
for blogs, emails, documents, or websites.
</p>

<h3>Popular Text Formatting Features</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Remove extra spaces</li>
  <li>Trim leading and trailing spaces</li>
  <li>Remove empty lines</li>
  <li>Add line breaks</li>
  <li>Format paragraphs properly</li>
</ul>

<p>
Using a text formatter saves time and ensures your content looks clean and polished.
</p>

<h2>What Is a Slug Converter?</h2>

<p>
A <strong>Slug Converter</strong> transforms normal text into a URL-friendly format,
commonly used in blog URLs and web pages.
</p>

<p>
Slugs are important for <strong>SEO</strong> because they make URLs readable for both
users and search engines.
